Motorcycle Piston Rings

We supply precision-engineered piston rings for motorcycles, scooters, ATVs, and small engines from 50cc to 1000cc. Available in chrome-plated, nitrided steel, and ductile cast iron.

Compatible with major brands including Honda, Yamaha, Suzuki, Kawasaki, Bajaj, TVS, Hero, and many Chinese brands. OEM specifications and custom sizes available.

  • Bore diameter: 39mm – 100mm (50cc–1000cc)
  • Chrome-plated top rings for wear resistance
  • Nitrided steel & ductile cast iron options
  • Compression rings & oil control rings
  • OEM spec or custom dimensions
  • Complete ring sets (2-ring & 3-ring sets)
