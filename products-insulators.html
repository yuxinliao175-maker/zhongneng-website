Electrical Insulators

We supply a comprehensive range of electrical insulators for power transmission and distribution networks. All products meet international IEC and ANSI standards, with full type test reports available.

Our insulator range covers porcelain, polymer/composite, glass, and hybrid designs for voltages from 11kV to 500kV.

  • Porcelain Disc & Suspension Insulators
  • Polymer / Composite Insulators (Silicone Rubber)
  • Pin Type & Post Insulators
  • Strain & Dead-End Insulators
  • IEC 60383 / ANSI C29.1 Certified
  • Voltage range: 11kV – 500kV
