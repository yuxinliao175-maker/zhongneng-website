Our Story

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in Liling — China's ceramic capital — Zhongneng Trading began as a specialized ceramics exporter. Over the years, we expanded our portfolio to include electrical insulators, LCD display modules, and motorcycle engine components.

Today, we serve clients in over 50 countries across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and the Americas. Our mission is simple: deliver quality products at fair prices, backed by honest service.
